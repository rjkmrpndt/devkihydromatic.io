GAMMA RADIATION: WHAT YOU NEED TO KNOW

Gamma radiation, generated by the decay of cobalt-60, is known for its high penetration capability. It facilitates the treatment of dense, bulk-packaged products. This process ensures complete sterility of products while maintaining product integrity.

Gamma Radiation

Benefits of Gamma Irradiation

  • Increased Shelf Life
  • Sterilization without Repacking
  • Clean, Gas-Free Process
  • High Throughput

Uses of Gamma Radiation

  • Sterilization
  • Shelf Life Extension
  • Insect Disinfection
  • Polymer Modification
  • Coloration of Gems
